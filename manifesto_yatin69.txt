Open Source Manifesto - 29 March 2026

I believe in the power of open source software.
Using tools like VLC player every day reminds me that technology grows stronger when it is shared.
For me, freedom in technology means expressiveness.
In the future, I would like to build and freely share Web apps so that others can learn, improve, and innovate together.
Open source encourages collaboration, transparency, and community-driven development.
